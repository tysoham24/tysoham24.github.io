    Ira ↗

    hybrid knowledge-graph RAG

    Pure vector search doesn't understand relationships and pure knowledge graphs miss paraphrase. Ira is a retrieval system over a Neo4j graph of retail transactions that combines guardrailed LLM-generated Cypher queries with dense embedding search over product descriptions, fusing the two rankings via Reciprocal Rank Fusion. FastAPI service, interactive Cytoscape.js graph dashboard, docker-composed with local Neo4j and Ollama; hybrid retrieval outperforms either strategy alone on precision@k and MRR across a 30-query gold set, with latency dominated by ~1s p50 LLM Cypher generation.

    neo4j · rag · fastapi · cytoscape.js · ollama
